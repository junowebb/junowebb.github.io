Trico: the Friendly Menace

About Trico

This fluffy boy loved almost everyone because he saw them as an opportunity to gain affection and food. He was exceptional at hunting and torturing small rodents, and his favorite thing to do was to leave their intact hearts on my doorstep. He was taken away from me years ago (it's a long story), but I still have hundreds of pictures of him and his orange (crazy) brother, Milo. I miss them both dearly every day.

About His Owner

His owner, me, received Trico and Milo when I was 14ish years old. They were birthed by the renowned cat named "Porch Cat" who belonged to my older sister. My little buddies and I made countless memories together for the 4 years I had them. They always greeted me by jumping on my car when I came home from school. They were quirky cats, but what cat isn't, really.
